All your Facebook events in one place.

UCPool allows you to get all the information you need from your Facebook events without having to switch between apps.

Mobile first.

UCPool is built primarily for mobile devices, so you can access information about your events, request or offer rides, and check all of your past or future rides at any time, wherever you are.

Get notified for ride-sharing opportunities.

If you need a ride to an event, we will let you know when we have found you a ride. If you are able to offer rides, we will put you in contact with like-minded people who need rides.

We arrange rides based on mutual interests.

UCPool uses data from Facebook to match you with people with whom you share many interests, allowing you to meet interesting people who like the same things you do.
